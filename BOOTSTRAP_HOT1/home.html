Why Buy Our Socks?

Everyone needs socks. We all wear them! But why settle for boring socks, when you could express your personality & style right on you feet! Add a pop of color to any outfit, or maybe you are feeling wild so a crazy pattern will help you express yourself.

Our Story

We employ all local artist. Some are more well known artist, others are from local high schools and community art shows. We believe in building community and giving people of all ages opportunities. We believe art is a vital part of life and want to incorporate it in every way we can.

Give Back and Get Back

For every pair of socks you buy, we donate a pair to a person in need. So every time you put on a pair you can know another person won't have cold feet because of your purchase. We believe it is important to help our neighbors in every way we can. You can even join us on our community sock give away day to see all our donated socks given out.
